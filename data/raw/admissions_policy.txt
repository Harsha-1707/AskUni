ANURAG UNIVERSITY - ADMISSIONS POLICY 2024-25

ABOUT ANURAG UNIVERSITY
Anurag University is a premier institution established in 2008, offering world-class education in Engineering, Management, and Sciences. Located in Hyderabad, Telangana, the university is accredited by NAAC with an 'A+' grade and approved by AICTE and UGC.

UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS (B.Tech)

Eligibility Criteria:
- Minimum 75% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in 10+2
- Valid score in ANURAG-ET (Anurag Entrance Test) or JEE Main
- Age limit: 17-25 years as of December 31, 2024

Available Specializations:
1. Computer Science & Engineering
2. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
3. Electronics & Communication Engineering
4. Mechanical Engineering
5. Civil Engineering
6. Information Technology
7. Data Science
8. Cyber Security

Admission Process:
1. Online application through www.anurag.edu.in (Opens: March 1, 2024)
2. ANURAG-ET examination (May 15, 2024) or JEE Main score submission
3. Counseling sessions (June 1-30, 2024)
4. Seat allocation based on merit
5. Document verification and fee payment
6. Final admission confirmation

POSTGRADUATE ADMISSIONS (M.Tech / MBA)

M.Tech Eligibility:
- B.Tech/B.E. degree with minimum 60% aggregate
- Valid GATE score or ANURAG-PGCET score
- Specializations: AI, ML, VLSI Design, Software Engineering, IoT

MBA Eligibility:
- Bachelor's degree in any discipline with 50% aggregate
- Valid CAT/MAT/GMAT/CMAT score
- Work experience preferred but not mandatory
- Specializations: Finance, Marketing, HR, Operations, Business Analytics

ADMISSION QUOTAS
- Merit Quota: 85%
- Management Quota: 10%
- NRI/Foreign National Quota: 5%

RESERVATION POLICY
As per Government of Telangana norms:
- SC: 15%
- ST: 7%
- OBC: 27%
- EWS: 10%

IMPORTANT DATES

Results Announcement: May 25, 2024
Counseling: June 1-30, 2024
