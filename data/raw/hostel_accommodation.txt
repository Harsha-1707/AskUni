ANURAG UNIVERSITY - HOSTEL & ACCOMMODATION POLICIES

HOSTEL FACILITIES

Anurag University provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities and 24/7 security. Our hostels are designed to provide a home away from home experience.

HOSTEL BLOCKS
Boys Hostel: 3 blocks (A, B, C) - Capacity: 1,200 students
Girls Hostel: 2 blocks (D, E) - Capacity: 800 students

ROOM TYPES & AMENITIES

AC Single Occupancy
- Bed with mattress, study table, chair, wardrobe
- Attached bathroom with geyser
- Air conditioning
- High-speed Wi-Fi (50 Mbps)
- Daily housekeeping
- Laundry service (paid)

Non-AC Double Occupancy
- Two beds with mattresses
- Two study tables and chairs
- Shared wardrobe
- Attached bathroom
- Ceiling fan
- Wi-Fi connectivity
- Housekeeping twice a week

Non-AC Triple Occupancy
- Three beds with mattresses
- Three study tables
- Common wardrobe
- Attached bathroom
- Ceiling fan
- Basic Wi-Fi
- Weekly housekeeping

COMMON FACILITIES
- Common rooms with TV and indoor games
- Reading room (open 24/7)
- Gym and fitness center
- Prayer room
- Washing machines (coin-operated)
- Water purifier on each floor
- Power backup
- CCTV surveillance

HOSTEL RULES & REGULATIONS

Discipline:
1. Hostel timings: Entry before 9:00 PM mandatory
2. Late permission only with prior approval from Warden
3. No visitors allowed in rooms after 7:00 PM
4. Ragging is strictly prohibited - liable for expulsion
5. Maintenance of cleanliness is mandatory

Security:
1. Entry/exit register maintained at gate
2. Valid ID card required for entry
3. Biometric attendance system
4. Parents/guardians must register at reception
5. Visitors allowed only in visitor's lounge (10 AM - 6 PM)

Prohibited Items:
- Electrical appliances (except laptops, mobile chargers)
- Alcohol, tobacco, and illegal substances
- Weapons of any kind
- Pets

Internet Usage:
- High-speed Wi-Fi available
- Filtering enabled for inappropriate content
- Speed throttling after 11:00 PM (academic sites exempted)
- Usage monitored for security

HOSTEL ALLOCATION

Priority Order:
1. Final year students
2. Distance from home (> 100 km gets priority)
3. Merit in previous semester
4. First-come-first-serve basis

Application Process:
1. Login to student portal
2. Fill hostel application form
3. Pay hostel fees
4. Room allocation within 7 days
5. Report to hostel with allotment letter

CHANGING ROOMS
- Room change requests accepted only twice per year
- Valid reason required (medical/emergency)
- ₹1,000 processing fee for room change
- Subject to availability

VACATION & HOLIDAYS
- Hostel open during mid-semester breaks
- Mandatory vacation during summer (May-June)
- Special permission for summer internship stay
- Extra charges: ₹500/day during official holidays

HEALTH & MEDICAL
- 24/7 medical room with nurse
- Tie-up with nearby hospital (1 km)
- Ambulance service available
- Monthly health check-ups
- First aid kits in each block
